About Muttom Oil Mills

Nestled in the heart of Kerala, Muttom Oil Mills has been a cornerstone of quality coconut oil production for generations. Our journey began in the picturesque village of Karimpanakulam, where our family's passion for producing the purest coconut oil was born.

For decades, we have upheld the tradition of cold-pressing coconuts to extract oil of the highest quality. Our commitment to natural processes ensures that every drop of Muttom coconut oil retains its authentic flavor, aroma, and nutritional benefits.

At Muttom Oil Mills, we believe in more than just producing oil; we're preserving a legacy. Our close ties with local farmers and our dedication to sustainable practices have made us an integral part of the community. We take pride in supporting local livelihoods while delivering a product that brings the essence of Kerala to homes across India and beyond.

Today, as we continue to grow, our core values remain unchanged. Every bottle of Muttom coconut oil is a testament to our family's dedication to quality, purity, and tradition. We invite you to experience the difference that generations of expertise can make in every drop of our oil.
